CARRIER GUIDANCE & PLACEMENT

GSS College has a Career Guidance Cell which provides information to students regarding employment opportunities available. They also help students to identify suitable jobs and job profiles. The college is on the list of leading companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, SAP, Accenture, Persistent, Concentrix, IBM, DXC, Cognizant etc. for campus interviews. Our students are invited for campus interviews and many have been selected for jobs at these companies
