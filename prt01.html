ABOUT BCA

WHY BCA @GSS

Govindram Seksaria Science College affiliated to Rani Channamma University, Belagavi offers the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) degree. From the academic year 2020 - 21, we follow the CBCS pattern as introduced by the Rani Channamma University.

1. Spacious multimedia aided classrooms.

2. Computer labs with latest configuration systems.

3. Dedicated, Professional staff with ideal blend of industry and academic experience.

4. Placement assistance and campus interviews.

5. Personality and soft skill development programmes.

6. Study tours, in-house activities, orientation programmes, technical fests, cultural events, workshops and many more.

GSS BCA is committed to develop professionals, trained in computer applications to meet the current needs of IT industry and inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship in Computer Applications

CARRIER GUIDANCE & PLACEMENT

GSS College has a Career Guidance Cell which provides information to students regarding employment opportunities available. They also help students to identify suitable jobs and job profiles. The college is on the list of leading companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, SAP, Accenture, Persistent, Concentrix, IBM, DXC, Cognizant etc. for campus interviews. Our students are invited for campus interviews and many have been selected for jobs at these companies
